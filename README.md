# Win - Where in Algeria

Win (Where in Algerian language) is a React Native mobile application designed to help travelers and explorers discover new and exciting places in Algeria. Whether you're a tourist or a local, Win provides you with a user-friendly platform to explore hidden gems, popular landmarks, and cultural experiences across Algeria.

![Win App Screenshot](screenshot.png)

## Features

- **Explore**: Discover a wide range of places, including restaurants, historical sites, parks, and more.
- **Search**: Easily find specific locations or types of places using our intuitive search feature.
- **Reviews and Ratings**: Read and leave reviews and ratings for places you visit.
- **User Profiles**: Create and manage your user profile, keep track of your favorite places, and share your experiences.
- **Location-Based**: Get recommendations based on your current location.
- **Offline Access**: Download maps and information for offline use when you're on the go.
